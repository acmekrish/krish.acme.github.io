WHO I AM

Idowu Israel O.A

Krish Acme

Born in Nigeria in 2000. After leaving secondary school and making it to covenant university, I never dreamed of becoming a content creator, but once in a while I imagined movies in my head and creative ideas which I would never actually try to sketch(Forgot to mention, I wasn't much of an artist then). Started going deep into design in 2018 when I got a phone call from a friend that changed my life, and once it became my passion, I found myself breaking limits of creativity like a daily hobby.
